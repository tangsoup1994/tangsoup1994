# 03 固定搭配

动词与介词的搭配、固定短语。介词是 Part 5 高频考点，重点记「哪个词配哪个介词」。

| 搭配 / 短语 | 含义 | 题中例句（英文原文） | 备注 |
|-------------|------|----------------------|------|
| prior to + 名词 | 在……之前（= before） | ten business days prior to the scheduled session | 介词短语，后接名词；Part 5 常用它替换 before |
| in case / in case of | in case + 句子、in case of + 名词：以防、万一 | —（本题干扰项 A） | 是连词 / 介词短语，义为「以防」，与「之前」无关，别和 prior to 混 |
| rapid expansion | 快速扩张 | has seen rapid expansion in East Asia | 形容词 + 名词搭配；说扩张速度快，不用 bright / loud / proud |
| due to + 名词 | 由于，因为 | due to the popularity of its clothing lines；Due to the heavy rain this morning | `due to` 后接名词或名词短语；如果后面是完整句子才用 because / while 等连词 |
| put + 宾语 + first | 把……放在首位，优先考虑…… | put quality service and customer care first | 商务服务理念常用表达；`first` 表优先级 |
| suggest doing sth | 建议做某事（suggest 后接动名词，不接 to do） | the director suggested filming the scene inside the studio | filming 是 suggest 的动名词宾语 |
| work in the industry | 在该行业工作 | have worked in the industry for many years | `industry` 前用 `the` 指特定行业；现在完成时强调经验延续到现在 |
| heavy rain | 大雨 | the heavy rain this morning | `heavy` 可修饰 rain / traffic / workload 等，表示程度大 |
| experience delays | 出现 / 遭遇延误 | the city’s subway system is experiencing delays | 交通、物流、项目进度场景常用；主语通常是系统、航班、服务或项目 |
| so + adj. + that + 句子 | 如此……以至于…… | so powerful that it can cut sheets of metal | `so` 修饰形容词 / 副词，`that` 后接结果句 |
| up to + 数量 | 多达，最高达到 | up to one centimeter thick | 用于说明上限、规格或能力范围 |
| financial specialist | 财务专员 / 财务专家 | financial specialist, Richard Bigsby | `financial` 修饰表示人的职位名词 `specialist` |
| handle budgetary decisions | 处理预算决策 | handles all budgetary decisions | `handle` 可表示负责处理事务、决策、问题 |
| be excited to announce that + 句子 | 很高兴宣布…… | is excited to announce that this month’s guest speaker is Joshua Ramsey | 公告、活动通知常用开头，`that` 后接完整句子 |
| expert in + 领域 | 某领域的专家 | an expert in climate science | `expert` 指人；`in` 后接专业领域 |
| environmental agency | 环保机构，环境机构 | a new environmental agency | `environmental` 修饰机构类型 |
| be established to do sth | 被成立来做某事 | a new environmental agency established to address the city’s pollution problem | 常用过去分词 `established` 后接不定式表目的 |
| address a problem | 处理 / 解决问题 | address the city’s pollution problem | `address` 在商务和公共事务语境中常表示着手处理问题 |
| be expected to do sth | 被期望 / 应该做某事 | Each employee is expected to use their designated parking space | 规章、通知、岗位要求中常用，语气比 must 稍缓 |
| designated parking space | 指定停车位 | their designated parking space | `designated` 作形容词，说明停车位已被分配或指定 |
| upward trend in sales | 销售额上升趋势 | The current upward trend in sales | `in sales` 说明趋势发生在销售数据上 |
| be likely to do sth | 很可能做某事 | is likely to continue through the upcoming holiday season | `likely` 后接不定式，表示可能性 |
| continue through + 时间段 | 持续贯穿某段时间 | continue through the upcoming holiday season | `through` 接时间段，强调一直持续到该期间结束 |
