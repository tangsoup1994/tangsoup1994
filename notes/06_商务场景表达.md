# 06 商务场景表达

邮件、会议、合同等商务场景的高频表达，按场景归类，方便按情境复习。

| 场景 | 表达（英文原文） | 含义 / 用法 | 题中例句 |
|------|------------------|-------------|----------|
| 客户服务 | put quality service and customer care first | 把优质服务和客户关怀放在首位，常用于公司服务理念或品牌承诺 | Here at Auto Empire, we always put quality service and customer care first. |
| 零售门店 | retail locations | 零售点、门店、线下销售地点；公司谈门店规模或网点分布时常用 | all the company’s retail locations |
| 教育培训 | active professionals | 仍在行业中活跃工作的专业人士，常用于说明教师、讲师或顾问具备实务经验 | are active professionals who have worked in the industry for many years |
| 交通通知 | subway system is experiencing delays | 地铁系统正在出现延误，常用于公共交通、通勤或运营通知 | the city’s subway system is experiencing delays |
| 产品性能 | can cut sheets of metal up to one centimeter thick | 能切割厚达一厘米的金属板，用于说明工具、机器或设备性能 | it can cut sheets of metal up to one centimeter thick |
| 财务职责 | handles all budgetary decisions | 负责处理所有预算决策，常用于说明财务、管理岗位职责 | Richard Bigsby, handles all budgetary decisions |
| 活动通知 | is excited to announce that this month’s guest speaker is ... | 很高兴宣布本月嘉宾演讲者是……，用于学校、会议或活动公告 | is excited to announce that this month’s guest speaker is Joshua Ramsey |
| 环境治理 | address the city’s pollution problem | 处理 / 解决城市污染问题，常用于政府、机构或环保项目说明 | established to address the city’s pollution problem |
| 员工规定 | use their designated parking space | 使用自己的指定停车位，常用于公司内部规定或设施使用说明 | Each employee is expected to use their designated parking space |
| 销售趋势 | upward trend in sales is likely to continue | 销售额上升趋势很可能继续，用于销售报告、业绩预测或市场分析 | The current upward trend in sales is likely to continue through the upcoming holiday season |
