# 02 高频词汇

单词的词性、释义、来自题目的例句，以及易混词提示。已记过的词只在「易混词 / 提示」或例句列补充，不重复建行。

| 单词 | 词性 | 释义 | 题中例句（英文原文） | 易混词 / 提示 |
|------|------|------|----------------------|----------------|
| business day | n. | 工作日（不含周末与节假日） | at least ten business days prior to the scheduled session | 不是「商务日」；表期限常与 prior to / before / after 搭配 |
| rapid | adj. | 快速的，迅速的 | has seen rapid expansion in East Asia | 常修饰 expansion / growth / change 等表示发展变化的名词；本题不能用 bright |
| expansion | n. | 扩张，扩大，发展 | rapid expansion in East Asia | 商业语境常指市场、业务、门店、地区覆盖的扩大 |
| popularity | n. | 受欢迎，流行 | due to the popularity of its clothing lines | `due to + 名词` 表原因；popularity 说明扩张原因，不决定选 bright |
| consecutive | adj. | 连续的，接连的 | After several consecutive days of delays due to bad weather | 常修饰 days / years / wins 等表数量或时间的名词 |
| delay | n./v. | 延误，推迟 | several consecutive days of delays due to bad weather；the city’s subway system is experiencing delays | 作名词时可数，常用复数 delays 表多处 / 多次延误；作动词时搭配 delay doing |
| professional | n./adj. | 专业人士；专业的 | are active professionals who have worked in the industry for many years | 作名词指有专业技能或从业经验的人；本题用复数 professionals |
| industry | n. | 行业，产业 | worked in the industry for many years | `the industry` 指前文语境中的行业；常与 work in 搭配 |
| saw | n. | 锯 | Our new electric saw is so powerful | `electric saw` 是电锯；不要误看成 see 的过去式 saw |
| powerful | adj. | 强劲的，功率大的 | so powerful that it can cut sheets of metal | 产品性能语境中常形容机器、工具、设备能力强 |
| metal | n. | 金属 | sheets of metal up to one centimeter thick | `sheets of metal` 指金属板 / 金属片 |
| financial | adj. | 财务的，金融的 | The Langdon Company’s financial specialist | 常修饰 report / decision / specialist / department 等财务相关名词 |
| specialist | n. | 专员，专家 | financial specialist, Richard Bigsby | 表示具有专门职责或专业知识的人；词尾 `-ist` 常指人 |
| budgetary | adj. | 预算的 | handles all budgetary decisions | 常修饰 decisions / constraints / planning 等预算相关名词 |
| announce | v. | 宣布，告知 | is excited to announce that this month’s guest speaker is Joshua Ramsey | 后面常接 `that + 句子` 表宣布的内容 |
| guest speaker | n. | 嘉宾演讲者，特邀讲者 | this month’s guest speaker is Joshua Ramsey | 学校、会议、活动通知中的常见表达 |
| expert | n./adj. | 专家；熟练的 | an expert in climate science | 作名词指人，是可数名词；本题受 `an` 限定用单数 |
| expertise | n. | 专业知识，专长 | —（本题干扰项 A） | 不可数抽象名词，不指专家本人；不能写成 `an expertise in ...` |
| environmental | adj. | 环境的，环保的 | a new environmental agency | 常修饰 agency / policy / issue / impact 等环境相关名词 |
| agency | n. | 机构，部门 | a new environmental agency established to address the city’s pollution problem | 可指政府、公共或专业机构 |
| establish | v. | 成立，建立 | a new environmental agency established to address the city’s pollution problem | 本题用过去分词 `established` 作后置定语，表示被成立 |
| pollution | n. | 污染 | the city’s pollution problem | 不可数名词；作定语时可用于 `pollution problem` |
| employee | n. | 员工，雇员 | Each employee is expected to use their designated parking space | 指受雇于公司的人员，区别于 employer「雇主」 |
| designate | v. | 指定，选定 | their designated parking space | 本题用过去分词 `designated` 作形容词，表示「被指定的」 |
| parking space | n. | 停车位 | designated parking space | 也可说 parking spot；公司场景常涉及员工停车位 |
| trend | n. | 趋势，走向 | The current upward trend in sales | 常与 upward / downward / current 搭配，表示数据变化方向 |
| upward | adj. | 向上的，上升的 | upward trend in sales | 常修饰 trend / movement，表示数据或数量上升 |
| sales | n. | 销售额，销售 | upward trend in sales | 商务语境中常指销售额或销售表现 |
| upcoming | adj. | 即将到来的 | the upcoming holiday season | 常修饰 event / meeting / season / launch 等 |
